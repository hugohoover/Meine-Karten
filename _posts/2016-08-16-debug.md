出現了`WARN: Unresolved specs during Gem::Specification.reset:`，於是到[stack overflow](http://stackoverflow.com/questions/17936340/unresolved-specs-during-gemspecification-reset)上找答案。

運行了這些命令，

    gem cleanup rouge
    gem cleanup jekyll-watch
    gem cleanup listen
    gem cleanup ffi
    bundle install

It works in my case.
